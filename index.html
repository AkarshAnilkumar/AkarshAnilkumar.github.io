    This winter season we are here with BEMAP ,a series of workshops and events not just for the Computer science department but also for
    B- Biotechnology
    E-Electronics
    M-Mechanical
    A-Automobile
    P-Production
    A 3-day program to bring together all the branches of engineering through various events and workshops. BEMAP aims to celebrate the spirit of togetherness by bringing various branches of engineering together.

    #SCT_IS_TOGETHER

    BEMAP aims to celebrate the unity in SCTCE to celebrate the beauty of engineering giving equal privilege to all of its dimensions. It celebrates the surprises we have seen , the ones we are yet to discover and path that it takes you on.

    About CSI

    Computer Society Of India, Sree Chitra Thirunal College Of Engineering Student Branch aims to develop students' technical skills for their professional development by providing numerous webinars and workshops on leading topics and striving to create a great platform to showcase your technical skillsets and knowledge.
